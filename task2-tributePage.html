Sunil Chhetri

Indian Football Legend

About Sunil Chhetri

Sunil Chhetri is an Indian professional footballer who currently serves as the captain of the Indian national team. He is regarded as one of the greatest footballers in Indian history.

Chhetri has achieved numerous milestones in his career and is known for his incredible goal-scoring ability.

Notable Achievements

  • Most international goals scored by an active player.
  • Padma Shri, India's fourth-highest civilian award.
  • Arjuna Award for his contribution to Indian football.
